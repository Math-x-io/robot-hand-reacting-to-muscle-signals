****ENGLISH****

How to get the parts to create your mechanical hand ? 

- First and easiest solution is to buy directly a hand model, then you will only have to wire it with the Arduino. 

Buy here : 

- https://www.gotronic.fr/art-main-droite-robotique-rob0143-27831.html  

 shorturl.at/bdL12

 The second solution is to either print the 3d files provided with a 3d printer (for materials any one should be good) 
 or use a laser cut on a flat surface material (use the PDF files provided).
